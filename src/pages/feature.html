Workflow management

Fielda Workflow management is optimized for efficiency. It simplifies your processes with rules and validations and reduces opportunities for human errors. Customize extensively and assign your activities to various stages as it fits your workflow and ‘To do’ to ‘Closed’ and everything in between.

Project management

Intelligent project management that prioritizes and notifies associated managers and engineers. With workflows driven by events and triggers, managing projects just got a whole lot easier than any other field service management software.

Collect unique data

Every project team is different. Track information that’s unique to your project, process, or organization. You can capture uncommon information about your project’s status, process, risk, or classification. Also create reports on metrics that are unique to your project or business with custom fields.

Add unique processes

No more generic forms. Customize your forms extensively with a variety of custom fields to create punch lists, inspection forms and data collection forms that are useful for your office or out in the field. Capture all the information you need, exactly the way you need.

Integrate with apps

Import data from Google Sheets, Microsoft Online, or your IT department databases and APIs. Connect external systems with Fielda to make them more useful to field workers on the go. Integrate with 3rd party apps to save time and enhance efficiency.

No more incorrect records

Always ensure that the data users enter in your forms meet the standards you specified before the forms can be submitted. Reduce human errors and missing data. Update activities automatically based on the rules you set.

Real-time Business Intelligence

Comprehensive dashboards with real-time data to help you make better decisions and make the most of your time and resources. Take action based on real-time data. Know who is working, who is online, and what they have assigned. Track location, and dispatch the work based on proximity.

Set up new engineers in minutes!

Add new workers and give them access to the projects and data they need. Fielda takes minutes to learn, not hours or days.

GPS Breadcrumbs

Enable GPS Breadcrumbs to locate engineers and also track the trail taken by them while working on various activities.

Collect access roads

Keep track of remote and access challenged routes with Fielda and ease access to your field engineers.

Tree Clearance

Manage your tree clearance on the road, and even charge customers directly at the job site!.
