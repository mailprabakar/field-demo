Team.

Pillars Of Strength. The Fielda Family.

Babu Krishnasamy

President/CEO

Babu brings in more than 2 decades of industry experience in developing software solutions for engineering, transportation, energy & utilities, government and financial services industries. He provides strategic direction to Fielda and leads business development. He is obsessed with maintaining the highest levels of customer satisfaction and believes Fielda's success is directly tied to its customer service.

Robert Claypool

Chief Technology Officer

Robert has 16 years of experience in software development and spatial data analysis. Since 2012, he has managed our Windows and Linux cloud infrastructure on AWS and Azure, and as Director of Applications Development, he oversees systems architecture, security, and DevOps practices across the organization. Robert is still a developer at heart; when issues arise, he likes to know exactly what the system is doing and work alongside other developers to implement a fix.

Barry Beckner

Chief Revenue Officer

Barry has over 2 decades of experience in technology sales operations, enablement, and strategy. At Fielda, Barry will be leading a customer growth strategy to ensure the company delivers a superlative experience for customers while accelerating revenue growth. He is passionate about improving field business operations through technology. Barry will focus on optimizing sales processes, building and empowering customer-focused teams to maximize value, recognize new revenue streams, and deliver technology solutions that have a profoundly positive impact on our customers business.

Karthik Subramanian

Chief Marketing Officer

Karthik brings over 16 years of experience in diverse marketing functions, including branding, communications, demand generation, field marketing, and product marketing. He has a proven track record of delivering maximum ROI from Marketing spends while executing integrated marketing plans from start to finish. As the Chief Marketing Officer at Fielda, he will be focused on brand building through digital and Go-to-Market strategies, ensuring double-digit growth.
