No paper trails. Stay real-time, stay mobile

Fielda combines the power of geospatial maps with highly customized forms to enable managing activities and your assets efficiently. The maps and the smart workflows provide accurate data on your assets and the insights to maintain them all from one place.

Choose customized workflow steps that cover activities end to end. Reduce human error with rules and validations at each step. Associate each step with a status like ‘To do’, ‘Scheduled’, ‘In Progress’, ‘Ready for Review’, and ‘Closed’.
